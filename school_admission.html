Admission Process

3

Review

Our admissions committee reviews the complete application

4

Decision

Receive admission decision and enrollment package

Admission Requirements

Documents Required

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate copy
  • Previous school reports
  • Passport-sized photos (4)
  • Medical health certificate

Age Requirements

  • Nursery: 3-4 years
  • Kindergarten: 5-6 years
  • Primary 1: 6-7 years
  • Primary 6: 11-12 years
  • Secondary: 13+ years

Assessment Criteria

  • Academic performance
  • Entrance examination
  • Interview with student
  • Parent meeting
  • Previous school reference

Programs by Age Group

3-4 Years

Early Years

Nursery & Preschool program focusing on foundational skills through play-based learning

5-11 Years

Primary School

Comprehensive primary education with focus on literacy, numeracy, and character building

12-18 Years

Secondary School

Advanced academic program preparing students for national examinations and beyond
