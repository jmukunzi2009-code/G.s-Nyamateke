About Our School

Our History & Legacy

Founded in 1995, G.S St Kizito Nyamateke has been a beacon of quality education in the Nyamateke sector. Named after Saint Kizito, the youngest of the Uganda Martyrs, our school embodies values of faith, courage, and dedication.

We have grown from a small primary school to a comprehensive educational institution offering primary education with strong emphasis on academic excellence, moral formation, and holistic development.

Our dedicated staff and supportive community work together to create an environment where every child can thrive and reach their full potential.

Our Guiding Principles

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students academically, spiritually, and socially, preparing them to become responsible citizens and future leaders grounded in Christian values.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, character formation, and holistic development of students who contribute positively to society.

Our Core Values

Excellence, Integrity, Respect, Responsibility, Faith, Compassion, Discipline, Teamwork - these values guide everything we do at G.S St Kizito Nyamateke.
