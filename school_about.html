Our Journey Through Time

From humble beginnings to becoming a beacon of educational excellence, trace our remarkable journey of growth and achievement.

1995

The Foundation

G.S NYAMATEKE was established with a vision to provide accessible, quality education to the local community, starting with basic primary education facilities.

Original campus with 3 classrooms

2002

First Major Expansion

Expanded our facilities to include science laboratories and computer rooms, becoming one of the first schools in the area with modern technology infrastructure.

Science lab established

2010

National Recognition

Received national recognition for academic excellence and community engagement, becoming a model for other schools in the region.

National Excellence Award

2020

Digital Transformation

Successfully implemented digital learning platforms while maintaining our commitment to holistic, student-centered education approach.

Digital classrooms launched

2023

Modern Campus

Completed state-of-the-art campus expansion with smart classrooms, sports complex, and innovation hub for 21st century learning.

New campus facilities opened

Our Core Values

These fundamental principles guide everything we do at G.S NYAMATEKE, shaping our community and educational approach.

Academic Excellence

We maintain high standards of teaching and learning, encouraging intellectual curiosity and critical thinking through proven pedagogical approaches.

  • Rigorous curriculum
  • Continuous assessment
  • Individual attention

Community Focus

We believe in building strong community ties and preparing students to be active, responsible members of society who contribute positively.

  • Parent engagement
  • Local partnerships
  • Social responsibility

Character Building

Beyond academics, we focus on developing integrity, empathy, resilience, and leadership qualities that prepare students for life's challenges.

  • Moral education
  • Leadership programs
  • Emotional intelligence
